What Roots does

Three things done exceptionally well.

Roots isn't a full church management system — and that's intentional. It does one thing: help your team know students deeply.

💬

Intentional Relationships

Log every conversation with a student — intensity, location, notes, prayer requests. Set reminders to follow up. Never lose track of where a student is spiritually.

  • Conversation logging with intensity levels
  • Student decisions & spiritual milestones
  • Follow-up reminders that email you on the day
  • Tag other students in conversations
  • Full student profiles with photos
📅

Organized Planning

Check students in and out of events, track attendance trends, and see who's been missing. Import from PushPay, Planning Center, or any CSV.

  • Event creation & attendance tracking
  • XLS & CSV check-in import
  • Workspace & personal calendar
  • Student subgroups & custom tags
  • Photo albums & event galleries
🤝

Teamwork & Collaboration

Keep your whole volunteer team in sync. Announcements, polls, prayer board, and a shared overview so the pastor can see everything.

  • Community chat & announcements
  • Polls & prayer board
  • Pastor overview dashboard
  • Email notifications for your team
  • Workspace invite links

Why I Built This

Roots got started because I was working with multiple youth ministry contexts like youth groups, YoungLife, and discipleship within my own friend group and I could never keep track of all of our conversations. So I thought there had to be a better way, and that's how Roots came to exist! Now, what exists is something completely more advanced than I ever thought could happen! Praise the Lord for this blessing and opportunity!

The Mission

Ministry Roots exists to help youth pastors and volunteers do intentional relational discipleship — the kind that changes lives — without getting buried in logistics.

We believe relational discipleship shouldn't require a big budget or a tech background. Every plan is designed around that conviction: start free, grow when you're ready, and never pay more than your ministry needs.

Tech & Transparency

Roots is built with React, Firebase, and deployed on Vercel. Student data is stored securely with strict access controls — your workspace data never leaves your team.

We handle data about minors with the seriousness it deserves. Privacy and security aren't an afterthought — they're foundational to how Roots is built.

Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start? +
Never for the Seed (free) plan. For Grove and Forest, you get a full 14-day free trial with no card required. You only pay when you decide to continue.
Can I switch plans later? +
Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. Your data is always yours and always safe.
Do you offer discounts for small or rural churches? +
Yes. We believe every church deserves tools built for their mission regardless of budget. Reach out to dylan.hovanec@gmail.com and we'll work something out.
Is student data secure? +
All data is stored securely with Firebase (Google's infrastructure), with email verification, app lock with Face ID/Touch ID, and strict access controls. Your student data never leaves your workspace.
What if I have more than 5 workspaces on Forest? +
Additional workspaces can be added to the Forest plan for $8/month each — no need to upgrade to a whole new tier.

The Mission

Ministry Roots exists to help youth pastors and volunteers do intentional relational discipleship — the kind that changes lives — without getting buried in logistics.

Tech & Transparency

Roots is built with React, Firebase, and deployed on Vercel. Student data is stored securely with strict access controls — your workspace data never leaves your team.

Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start? +
Never for the Seed (free) plan. For Grove and Forest, you get a full 14-day free trial with no card required. You only pay when you decide to continue.
Can I switch plans later? +
Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. Your data is always yours and always safe.
Do you offer discounts for small or rural churches? +
Yes. We believe every church deserves tools built for their mission regardless of budget. Reach out to dylan.hovanec@gmail.com and we'll work something out.

Additional workspaces can be added to the Forest plan for $8/month each — no need to upgrade to a whole new tier.

What Roots does

Three things done exceptionally well.

Roots isn't a full church management system — and that's intentional. It does one thing: help your team know students deeply.

💬

Intentional Relationships

Log every conversation with a student — intensity, location, notes, prayer requests. Set reminders to follow up. Never lose track of where a student is spiritually.

  • Conversation logging with intensity levels
  • Student decisions & spiritual milestones
  • Follow-up reminders that email you on the day
  • Tag other students in conversations
  • Full student profiles with photos
📅

Organized Planning

Check students in and out of events, track attendance trends, and see who's been missing. Import from PushPay, Planning Center, or any CSV.

  • Event creation & attendance tracking
  • XLS & CSV check-in import
  • Workspace & personal calendar
  • Student subgroups & custom tags
  • Photo albums & event galleries
🤝

Teamwork & Collaboration

Keep your whole volunteer team in sync. Announcements, polls, prayer board, and a shared overview so the pastor can see everything.

  • Community chat & announcements
  • Polls & prayer board
  • Pastor overview dashboard
  • Email notifications for your team
  • Workspace invite links

Why I Built This

Roots got started because I was working with multiple youth ministry contexts like youth groups, YoungLife, and discipleship within my own friend group and I could never keep track of all of our conversations. So I thought there had to be a better way, and that's how Roots came to exist! Now, what exists is something completely more advanced than I ever thought could happen! Praise the Lord for this blessing and opportunity!

The Mission

Ministry Roots exists to help youth pastors and volunteers do intentional relational discipleship — the kind that changes lives — without getting buried in logistics.

We believe relational discipleship shouldn't require a big budget or a tech background. Every plan is designed around that conviction: start free, grow when you're ready, and never pay more than your ministry needs.

Tech & Transparency

Roots is built with React, Firebase, and deployed on Vercel. Student data is stored securely with strict access controls — your workspace data never leaves your team.

We handle data about minors with the seriousness it deserves. Privacy and security aren't an afterthought — they're foundational to how Roots is built.

Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start? +
Never for the Seed (free) plan. For Grove and Forest, you get a full 14-day free trial with no card required. You only pay when you decide to continue.
Can I switch plans later? +
Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. Your data is always yours and always safe.
Do you offer discounts for small or rural churches? +
Yes. We believe every church deserves tools built for their mission regardless of budget. Reach out to dylan.hovanec@gmail.com and we'll work something out.
Is student data secure? +
All data is stored securely with Firebase (Google's infrastructure), with email verification, app lock with Face ID/Touch ID, and strict access controls. Your student data never leaves your workspace.
What if I have more than 5 workspaces on Forest? +
Additional workspaces can be added to the Forest plan for $8/month each — no need to upgrade to a whole new tier.
