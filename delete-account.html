Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

What gets deleted:

Your profile, display name, email address, profile photo, PIN, and all personal settings.

What happens to workspace data:

If you own workspaces, ownership must be transferred or the workspace will be deleted along with all student records, conversations, check-ins, events, and photos.

Subscriptions:

Please cancel any active subscriptions at ministryroots.com/pricing before requesting deletion. Account deletion does not automatically cancel subscriptions.

You will receive a confirmation email within 5 business days.
