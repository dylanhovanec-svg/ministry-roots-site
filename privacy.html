Ministry Roots ("Ministry Roots," "Roots," "we," "us," or "our") is a sole proprietorship operated by Dylan Hovanec that operates the Roots application available at ministryroots.com. We are committed to protecting the privacy of youth ministry leaders, volunteers, and the students whose information is managed through our platform.

By using Roots, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Roots is a youth ministry relationship-tracking platform designed exclusively for licensed youth ministry leaders, pastors, volunteers, and church organizations. Roots is operated by Ministry Roots, a sole proprietorship owned and operated by Dylan Hovanec.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Your name and email address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Display name and nickname
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Social media handles (optional)
  • A PIN you create for app security

Student / Minor Information

Roots is designed for youth ministry professionals to manage information about the students they serve. This may include information about minors under 18. Information stored about students may include:

  • Full name, nickname, date of birth, and grade
  • Phone number, email, school, and campus
  • Parent or guardian name and contact information
  • Profile photos and attendance / check-in records
  • Private conversation notes logged by ministry leaders
  • Spiritual decision records (e.g., salvation, baptism, rededication)
Important: Student information is entered exclusively by authorized ministry leaders. Roots does not collect this information directly from minors.

Workspace & Ministry Data

  • Workspace settings and member roster
  • Events, itineraries, and attendance records
  • Community chat messages, prayer requests, and polls
  • Photos uploaded to the workspace photo feed

Technical & Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system, and browser
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage patterns and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Roots platform
  • Enable ministry leaders to track relationships with students
  • Send email notifications about workspace activity and events
  • Authenticate users and enforce app security
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations
We do NOT use student or volunteer information for advertising, marketing, or sale to third parties.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not allow children to create accounts or directly submit personal information.

  • Ministry leaders are responsible for obtaining parental consent before entering student information.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.
  • If you believe a child's information has been entered without appropriate authorization, contact us and we will promptly remove it.

5. Data Storage & Security

Roots is built on Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard security including data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. The App also enforces:

  • Mandatory PIN protection for all users
  • Optional biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)
  • Automatic app locking when backgrounded or reopened

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — data hosting, authentication, storage
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Vercel — application hosting and deployment

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of individuals.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us. When a Workspace is deleted, all associated data is permanently removed.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request an export of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email dylan.hovanec@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes via email or in-app notice. Continued use of Roots after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
